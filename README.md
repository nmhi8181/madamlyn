# Madamlyn Research Folder

This folder contains a curated keynote research pack for the topic "Leadership, Social Impact & Building Businesses That Strengthen Communities" for the 16th World Leaders Summit - UK Chapter 2026 at Keble College, Oxford.

The pack currently includes 28 PDFs and 1 HTML snapshot, organized as follows:

- `docs`: GitHub Pages-ready interactive presentation deck.
- `sources/01_event_context`: summit context and event snapshot.
- `sources/02_frameworks`: core leadership, inclusive business, stakeholder, social innovation, and systems-change frameworks.
- `sources/03_case_studies`: community wealth building, social economy, inclusive business, and cooperative model case material.
- `sources/04_local_oxford`: Oxford and Oxfordshire material for local relevance.
- `notes/keynote_research_brief.md`: the main synthesis of findings.
- `notes/keynote_outline.md`: a practical 12-15 minute keynote structure.
- `notes/source_index.md`: a file-by-file guide with source URLs and suggested use.
- `notes/keynote_speech_complete.md`: full keynote manuscript with source citations.

Selection approach:

- I prioritized official reports, policy papers, and high-signal practitioner research over general blog posts.
- I focused on sources that connect leadership to operating models, jobs, procurement, ownership, partnerships, and measurable community outcomes.
- Where a publisher page was unstable, I saved the underlying PDF directly when possible.
