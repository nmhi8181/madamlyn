About Global Empire Events Limited

Global Empire Events Limited is a UK based international organisation driving leadership, innovation and global collaboration, registered with the Companies House in UK, specializes in organizing world class networking & corporate conferences, summits and exhibitions not just in The UK but also in UAE and India. Headquartered in London Paul Street, we as an organisation focus on delivering world class corporate conferences, with an aim to bridge the gap between potential and possibilities catering to provide a Global platform to HNI'S, Business Owners, Startups, Entrepreneurs and Education Leaders from over 30+ countries.
